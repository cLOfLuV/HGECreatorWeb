HGE Creator is Korea's leading game-creator collaboration community — connecting indie game developers with 1,000+ active streamers across CHZZK, YouTube, Twitch, SOOP, and CIMe.

Korean new-media is growing fast. Viewers trust streamers far more than ads. Our creators join collabs because they genuinely love the games — which means authentic content, real audience engagement, and measurable reach.

If you're a publisher or developer looking to break into the Korean market or tap into Korea's streamer-driven new-media ecosystem, we'd love to work together.
